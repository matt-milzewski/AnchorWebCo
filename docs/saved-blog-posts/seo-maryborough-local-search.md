<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">1. Build a Location Structure That Matches Real Demand</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-6">Many Fraser Coast businesses only target one location page. That leaves traffic on the table. A stronger approach is to run separate location pages for Maryborough and Hervey Bay with clear internal linking between both.</p>
<p class="text-lg mb-6">Start with <a href="/seo-maryborough.html" class="text-coast-sky font-medium hover:underline">SEO Maryborough</a> and <a href="/seo-hervey-bay.html" class="text-coast-sky font-medium hover:underline">SEO Hervey Bay</a>, then support those pages with service-specific content.</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">2. Prioritise Google Business Profile Optimisation</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-4">Local rankings are heavily influenced by profile quality. Focus on:</p>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li>Accurate categories and services.</li>
<li>Consistent NAP details across directories.</li>
<li>Service area relevance for Maryborough and nearby suburbs.</li>
<li>Consistent review acquisition and owner responses.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">3. Target Suburbs, Not Just City Terms</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-6">Use suburb language naturally within page copy and FAQ content where relevant. For Maryborough this often includes Granville, Tinana, Aldershot, Walkers Point, and Maryborough CBD.</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">4. Use Real Proof Content</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-6">Case-study pages and local project outcomes build trust quickly. For example, BH Lock & Security achieved top visibility for "locksmith Maryborough" with focused local SEO execution and content updates.</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">5. Track Conversion Signals Weekly</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-4">Do not rely on rankings alone. Track:</p>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li>Calls from organic search.</li>
<li>Contact form enquiries by landing page.</li>
<li>Map profile interactions and direction clicks.</li>
<li>Landing-page CTR changes in Search Console.</li>
</ul>
