<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">1. Start with Local Intent, Not Generic Design Trends</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-6">Maryborough businesses compete in a practical market. Customers want fast answers, trust signals, and clear next steps. Your website should prioritise what people actually need when they search for a local provider.</p>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li>Clear service areas across Maryborough, Hervey Bay, and the wider Fraser Coast.</li>
<li>Click-to-call and enquiry forms that are easy to use on mobile.</li>
<li>Simple service page structure with suburb references where relevant.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">2. Build Around Conversion Pages</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-6">A strong homepage is not enough. You need service pages, location pages, and clear proof content. If you want help building the structure properly, start with our <a href="/web-design-maryborough.html" class="text-coast-sky font-medium hover:underline">Web Design Maryborough service page</a>.</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">3. Use Local Proof, Not Generic Claims</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-4">The fastest way to improve trust is to show real local outcomes. Include:</p>
<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li>Project examples from Maryborough and Hervey Bay.</li>
<li>Specific before and after outcomes when available.</li>
<li>Industry relevance for tradies, tourism, professional services, and manufacturing support businesses.</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">4. Make SEO Part of the Build</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-6">Do not treat SEO as a bolt-on after launch. Good local websites include metadata structure, schema, page hierarchy, and internal linking from day one. If rankings are already a focus, pair your build with <a href="/seo-maryborough.html" class="text-coast-sky font-medium hover:underline">SEO Maryborough</a> or <a href="/seo-hervey-bay.html" class="text-coast-sky font-medium hover:underline">SEO Hervey Bay</a>.</p>
<p class="text-lg mb-6">For a deeper local ranking roadmap, read <a href="/blog/seo-maryborough-local-search/" class="text-coast-sky font-medium hover:underline">SEO Maryborough: How Fraser Coast Businesses Can Dominate Local Search</a>.</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">5. Prioritise Mobile Performance</h2>
<p class="text-lg mb-6">Google uses mobile-first indexing. Slow pages, oversized images, and poor button spacing can hurt both rankings and conversions. Keep the layout lightweight, readable, and conversion-focused.</p>
