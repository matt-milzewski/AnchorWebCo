<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">1. Complete Your Business Profile</h2>

<h3 class="text-xl font-poppins font-bold mb-4 text-anchor-navy">Essential Information</h3>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">Start with the basics that Google and your customers need:</p>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Business name:</strong> Use your exact business name as it appears on signage and legal documents</li>
<li><strong>Category selection:</strong> Choose the most specific category that describes your business</li>
<li><strong>Physical address:</strong> Include your complete street address for local search</li>
<li><strong>Service areas:</strong> Define where you provide services if you don't have a physical location</li>
<li><strong>Phone number:</strong> Use a local phone number that customers can call</li>
<li><strong>Website URL:</strong> Link to your main website or landing page</li>
</ul>

<div class="bg-sand-beige p-6 rounded-lg mb-6">
<h3 class="text-xl font-poppins font-bold mb-4 text-anchor-navy">📍 Local SEO Tip</h3>
<p class="text-lg">For Queensland businesses, include your suburb name in your business description and posts. This helps with local keyword targeting and improves visibility in specific areas.</p>
</div>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">2. Optimize Your Business Description</h2>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">Your business description is a crucial ranking factor and customer conversion tool:</p>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Include local keywords:</strong> Naturally incorporate location-specific terms</li>
<li><strong>Highlight unique services:</strong> Mention what sets you apart from competitors</li>
<li><strong>Use customer language:</strong> Write in terms your customers would use</li>
<li><strong>Include call-to-action:</strong> Encourage customers to contact or visit you</li>
<li><strong>Update regularly:</strong> Keep information current and relevant</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">3. High-Quality Photos and Videos</h2>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">Visual content significantly impacts GMB performance:</p>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Profile photo:</strong> Use your logo or a professional business image</li>
<li><strong>Cover photo:</strong> Show your business location or team in action</li>
<li><strong>Interior/exterior photos:</strong> Help customers recognize your business</li>
<li><strong>Product/service photos:</strong> Showcase what you offer</li>
<li><strong>Team photos:</strong> Put faces to your business name</li>
<li><strong>Video content:</strong> Short videos about your business or services</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">4. Business Hours and Special Hours</h2>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">Accurate hours are essential for customer trust and local SEO:</p>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Regular hours:</strong> Set your standard business hours</li>
<li><strong>Special hours:</strong> Update for holidays, events, or temporary changes</li>
<li><strong>Seasonal adjustments:</strong> Modify hours for different seasons if applicable</li>
<li><strong>Holiday hours:</strong> Set specific hours for public holidays</li>
<li><strong>Real-time updates:</strong> Change hours immediately when needed</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">5. Services and Products</h2>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">Detailed service listings improve local search visibility:</p>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Service categories:</strong> List all services you provide</li>
<li><strong>Service descriptions:</strong> Include detailed descriptions for each service</li>
<li><strong>Pricing information:</strong> Add price ranges where appropriate</li>
<li><strong>Product listings:</strong> Showcase specific products you sell</li>
<li><strong>Local service areas:</strong> Specify where you provide services</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">6. Customer Reviews and Responses</h2>

<h3 class="text-xl font-poppins font-bold mb-4 text-anchor-navy">Managing Reviews</h3>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">Reviews are crucial for local SEO and customer trust:</p>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Encourage reviews:</strong> Ask satisfied customers to leave reviews</li>
<li><strong>Respond to all reviews:</strong> Thank positive reviews and address negative ones</li>
<li><strong>Monitor regularly:</strong> Set up alerts for new reviews</li>
<li><strong>Address concerns:</strong> Respond professionally to negative feedback</li>
<li><strong>Highlight positive reviews:</strong> Share great reviews on your website</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-xl font-poppins font-bold mb-4 text-anchor-navy">Review Response Strategy</h3>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Thank reviewers:</strong> Always acknowledge positive feedback</li>
<li><strong>Address issues:</strong> Respond constructively to negative reviews</li>
<li><strong>Be professional:</strong> Maintain a professional tone in all responses</li>
<li><strong>Take action:</strong> Fix issues mentioned in negative reviews</li>
<li><strong>Follow up:</strong> Check back with customers who had issues</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">7. Regular Posts and Updates</h2>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">Regular posting keeps your GMB profile active and engaging:</p>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Weekly posts:</strong> Share updates, offers, or news</li>
<li><strong>Local events:</strong> Post about community involvement</li>
<li><strong>Seasonal content:</strong> Share relevant seasonal information</li>
<li><strong>Customer spotlights:</strong> Highlight customer success stories</li>
<li><strong>Behind-the-scenes:</strong> Show your team and business culture</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">8. Questions and Answers</h2>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">The Q&A section is often overlooked but highly valuable:</p>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Monitor questions:</strong> Check for new questions regularly</li>
<li><strong>Answer promptly:</strong> Respond to questions within 24 hours</li>
<li><strong>Provide detailed answers:</strong> Give comprehensive, helpful responses</li>
<li><strong>Use local keywords:</strong> Include location-specific terms in answers</li>
<li><strong>Encourage engagement:</strong> Ask follow-up questions when appropriate</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">9. Insights and Analytics</h2>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">Use GMB insights to optimize your local SEO strategy:</p>

<li><strong>Views and clicks:</strong> Track how many people see and interact with your profile</li>
<li><strong>Customer actions:</strong> Monitor website visits, calls, and directions requests</li>
<li><strong>Photo views:</strong> See which photos get the most attention</li>
<li><strong>Demographics:</strong> Understand your audience better</li>
</ul>
