What Happens to an Unmanaged Website

Most Fraser Coast business owners build their website and then never look at it again. That's understandable, you're busy. But websites require ongoing attention to stay healthy.

Plugins go out of date. Security vulnerabilities are discovered. Google's ranking requirements change. Hosting and SSL certificates lapse. The result? A slow, broken, or hacked site right before your peak season.

The most common problems we fix

  • Site goes down and the owner finds out from a customer.
  • Outdated plugins cause forms to stop working.
  • Site gets hacked and Google marks it as dangerous.
  • No backups, and a single mistake wipes weeks of content.
  • Page speed drops as files accumulate and nothing is cleaned up.

Care Plan FAQs

What is a website care plan?

A website care plan is ongoing support for your website so it stays online, supported, and easier to manage as your business grows.

Do I need a care plan if my site was just built?

Yes. Even brand-new websites need ongoing attention. Platforms and plugins update constantly, and an unpatched site can break or be compromised. A care plan means you never get caught out right before a busy period.

Can you maintain a site you didn't build?

Yes. We can take over the care and maintenance of your existing website regardless of who built it. We'll do an initial audit and onboarding to get familiar with your setup.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Our website support plans have no lock-in contract. Cancel or change your plan at any time with 30 days notice.
