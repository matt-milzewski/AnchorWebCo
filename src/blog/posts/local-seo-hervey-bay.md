<h2 class="text-2xl font-poppins font-bold mb-6 text-anchor-navy">1. Optimize Your Google My Business Profile</h2>

<p class="text-lg mb-4">Your Google My Business (GMB) profile is the foundation of local SEO success. Here's how to optimize it for Hervey Bay:</p>

<ul class="list-disc list-inside mb-6 space-y-2 text-lg">
<li><strong>Complete your profile:</strong> Fill out every section including business hours, services, and photos</li>
<li><strong>Use local keywords:</strong> Include "Hervey Bay" and relevant service terms in your description</li>
<li><strong>Add location-specific photos:</strong> Show your business, team, and local landmarks</li>
<li><strong>Encourage customer reviews:</strong> Positive reviews boost local rankings significantly</li>
<li><strong>Respond to reviews:</strong> Engage with customers to show you care about feedback</li>
</ul>
